## Role-based angles (for emails, demos, or landing blocks)

### Finance & accounting
- **Pain:** Manual P&Ls, copying from Excel to PowerPoint, errors in formulas.
- **Pitch:** *"Describe your P&L in words; Meldra builds the numbers and charts. Export to Excel or PowerPoint for month-end and board packs."*
- **Hook:** *"P&L from a sentence, not a spreadsheet."*

### Sales & operations
- **Pain:** Data in different formats, dirty filenames, slow reporting.
- **Pitch:** *"Upload CSVs or Excel, clean and analyze in one place, then turn it into slides or PDFs. No IT ticket, no formula training."*
- **Hook:** *"From messy data to client-ready slides in one tool."*

### Executives / decision-makers
- **Pain:** Need insights fast; don’t want to wait for analysts or risk sending data outside.
- **Pitch:** *"Ask questions about your data in plain English. Get charts and summaries. Your files never leave your machine—we don’t store anything."*
- **Hook:** *"Answers from your data. No storage, no spreadsheets."*

### Compliance, legal, risk
- **Pain:** Data residency, who sees the data, retention.
- **Pitch:** *"Processing happens in the user’s browser. We don’t store inputs or outputs. When the session ends, it’s all gone."*
- **Hook:** *"Use powerful analysis without sending data to our servers."*

## Quick demos that resonate with business users

1. **P&L from a sentence** — e.g. *"Revenue 100k, COGS 40%, OpEx 30k"* → P&L + chart.
2. **Excel to PowerPoint** — One Excel, one click → slides with charts and tables.

4. **Document conversion** — PDF ↔ Word, PPT ↔ PDF. *"No extra subscriptions or installs."*
5. **ZIP cleaner** — Messy ZIP with special characters → clean filenames, fewer support calls.
